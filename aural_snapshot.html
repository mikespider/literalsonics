Aural Snapshots

Capturing the sonic essence of a moment in time or a physical location.

Aural Snapshots are short sonic documents — fragments of time translated into sound. Each piece captures the acoustic identity of a place, an event, or a fleeting moment, preserving it not as a recording alone, but as an interpreted sonic structure.

Rather than reproducing reality, these works reshape it. Environmental sound, spatial resonance, and temporal patterns are distilled into compositions that sit between field recording, sound design, and abstraction.

Every snapshot is both a document and a transformation — a way of hearing space differently.

Urban Pulse

Rhythmic structures extracted from city movement and traffic flow.

Forest Resonance

Layered textures derived from wind, leaves, and spatial depth in woodland.
