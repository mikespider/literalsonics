How It Works

Earth's magnetic field data is mapped to spatial reverb, pitch delay, and reverse delay modulation in real time. The three-dimensional vector components (Bx, By, Bz) from scientific measurements drive every aspect of the processing — from reverb room size to reverse delay read position jitter.

Three selectable mapping modes allow you to reassign which magnetic parameter controls drift, jitter, and chunk size, giving you flexible control over how the planet's pulse shapes your sound.
