How It Works

The pitch delay is continuously modulated by Earth's magnetic field:

  • Left channel responds to magnetic angle
  • Right channel responds to turbulence
  • Mix responds to magnetic magnitude

This creates wide, evolving textures perfect for ambient and experimental sound design.
