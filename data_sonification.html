Data Sonification

Transforming numerical data, shapes, and movements into meaningful, musical experiences.

Exploring Data Through Sound

Data Sonification is the practice of translating information into auditory experiences, enabling us to perceive patterns, trends, and relationships through listening rather than visual inspection. At Literal Sonics, we map datasets into musical parameters such as pitch, rhythm, amplitude, and timbre.

This approach reveals structure that is often hidden in visual formats, turning data into something experiential rather than abstract.

Planetary Distances in Sound

One of our compositions maps planetary distances into frequency relationships, creating a harmonic model of the solar system.

Inner planets produce higher tones, while outer planets generate deeper resonances.
