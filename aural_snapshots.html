Aural Snapshots

Capturing the sonic essence of a moment in time or a physical location.

Aural Snapshots are short sonic documents — fragments of time translated into sound. Each piece captures the acoustic identity of a place, an event, or a fleeting moment.

Rather than reproducing reality, these works reshape it through spatial listening, abstraction, and transformation.

Coastal Drift

Tidal motion and wind interaction.

Urban Pulse

Rhythmic structures from city flow.

Forest Resonance

Layered natural spatial textures.
